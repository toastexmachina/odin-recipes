Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 bunch scallions (green onions), chopped
  • 1/2 cup kimchi, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 cup seafood (shrimp, squid, or a combination), chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Prepare Batter: In a mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of all-purpose flour and 2 cups of water. Add 1 large egg, 1 teaspoon of soy sauce, 1 teaspoon of sesame oil, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Whisk the ingredients together until you have a smooth batter.
  2. Add Scallions and Optional Ingredients: Chop 1 bunch of scallions (green onions) and add them to the batter. Optionally, add 1/2 cup of finely chopped kimchi and 1/2 cup of chopped seafood (shrimp, squid, or a combination) to the batter.
  3. Mix Well: Mix all the ingredients well, ensuring an even distribution of scallions and optional ingredients in the batter.
  4. Heat Vegetable Oil: In a large skillet or frying pan, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour Batter and Cook: Pour a ladle of batter into the hot pan, spreading it into a round shape. Cook until the edges become golden brown and crispy, and bubbles form on the surface.
  6. Flip and Cook: Carefully flip the pancake using a spatula and cook the other side until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Repeat: Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more oil to the pan as needed.
  8. Serve Warm: Serve the Pajeon warm, cut into slices, and optionally dip in a soy-based dipping sauce.
